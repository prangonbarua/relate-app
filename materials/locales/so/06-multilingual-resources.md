## Tixgelinta LEP iyo Akhriska

- 25.7 milyan oo qof (8% dadweynaha Maraykanka 5+) ayaa lahaansho aqoon Ingiriisi xaddidan ilaa 2021
- Luuqadaha LEP ee ugu sarreeya: Isbaanish (63%), Shiinees (7%), Fiyatnaamiis (3%), Carabi (2%), Tagalog (2%)
- Heerarka LEP ee ugu sarreeya: Burma (74%), Vietnamese (58%), Nepali (58%), Shiinaha (55%)
-Dadka LEP guud ahaan aqoontoodu aad bay u yar tahay waxayna aad ugu dhowdahay inay ku noolaadaan faqri; 20%+ ka hooseeya heerka faqriga
- 60% shakhsiyaadka LEP waxay ku urursan yihiin CA (25%), TX (14%), FL (9%), NY (9%)

**Tixgalinta akhris-qoraalka muhiimka ah ee abka:**
- Dadka ku hadla Haitian Creole inta badan ma akhriyi karaan mana qori karaan Creole in kasta oo ay ku hadlaan -- agabka qoran kuma filna
- Af-soomaaligu waxa uu leeyahay dhaqan qoraal ah oo kooban; Waxyaabo afka ah/video ku jira waa lama huraan
- Luuqadaha Koonfurta Aasiya waxay leeyihiin qoraalo kala duwan (Urdu waxay isticmaashaa far Carabi, Hindi waxay isticmaashaa Devanagari)
- Waxa ku jira maqal iyo muuqaal ayaa ka heli kara qoraalka kooxo badan

## Platforms Kheyraadka Luqadda Isku-tallaabta

### Ootiisamku wuxuu ku hadlaa agab aan Ingiriisi ahayn
Luqadaha: Carabi, Bangla, Bosnian, Cambodian, Chinese, French, Greek, Korean, Laotian, Portuguese, Ruush, Serbian, Somali, Spanish, Vietnamese. Dhawaan imaanaya: Jabbaan, Faaris, Romanian. Xirmada 100ka Maalmood waxaa lagu heli karaa Ingiriis, Isbaanish, iyo 17+ luqado kale oo bilaash ah. URL: [autismspeaks.org/non-english-resources](https://www.autismspeaks.org/non-english-resources)

### CDC "Baro Calaamadaha. Horey u dhaqan" (LTSAE)
Liisaska Hubinta Hal-abuurka ee Ingiriisi, Isbaanish, Shiine la Fududeeyay, Kuuriyaan, Fiyatnaamiis. Milestone Tracker App ee Ingiriisi iyo Isbaanish (iOS/Android). URL: [cdc.gov/act-early](https://www.cdc.gov/act-early/index.html)

### M-CHAT-R/F Qalabka Baadhista
Waxaa lagu turjumay 58 luqadood iyo lahjado. Noocyo la ansixiyay oo ku qoran Isbaanish, Shiine, Boortaqiis, Seerbiya, Maali, Waqooyiga Sotho, Albaniyan. Waxaa lagu heli karaa Carabi, Shiinees, Creole, Boortaqiis, Isbaanish (noocyo badan), Vietnamese. Fiiro gaar ah: dareenku wuxuu noqon karaa mid hooseeya ilaa 25% marka la eego tirakoobyada qaar inkastoo turjumaada adagi jirto, sababtoo ah caadooyinka dhaqameed ee ku xeeran aqoonsiga dabeecadaha inay yihiin kuwo aan caadi ahayn. Soo dejiso bilaash ah [mchatscreen.com](https://www.mchatscreen.com/mchat-rf/translations/)

### Ootiisamka Adduunka
Alaabooyinka Isbaanish, Boortaqiis, Fiyatnaamiis, Istooniyaan, Faransiis, Sawaaxili, Polish, Romanian, Talyaani, Haitian Creole. Tusaha kheyraadka caalamiga ah ee waddanku raadin karo. URL: [autismaroundtheglobe.org](https://www.autismaroundtheglobe.org/)

Ilaha Ingiriisi, Isbaanish, Shiine la Fududeeyay, Fiyatnaamiis, Faransiis, Ruush. Aflaanta dokumentiga gaarka ah ee dhaqanka Hisbaanik/Laatiino, African-American, iyo qoysaska Aasiya. URL: [phillyautismproject.org](https://www.phillyautismproject.org/multicultural-and-multilingual-resources/)

## Burburka Luqadda-Luqadda

### Isbaanishka (41.8M ku hadla)

** Aaladaha Daawashada:** M-CHAT-R/F (Noocyo badan oo Isbaanish ah oo la ansaxiyay), ASQ-3 oo Isbaanishka ah, CDC Milestone Checklists oo Isbaanish ah.

** Kheyraadka Dawladda:** CDC LTSAE agab Isbaanishka oo buuxa iyo App-ka Dabagalka Milestone ee Isbaanishka; Wakaaladaha dawliga ah ee CA, TX, FL, NY waxay leeyihiin ilaha autism ee Isbaanishka.

**Faa'iido-doon:** Ootiisamku wuxuu ku hadlaa maktabada kheyraadka Isbaanishka oo buuxda iyo khadka caawinta Isbaanishka (888) 772-9050; TACA (Ka hadal ku saabsan daaweynta Ootiisamka / Hablando de curar el autism); Colorin Colorado; Padres Palante (NJ).

**Buugaag/Video/Apps:** Ootiisamku waxa uu ku hadlaa 100 maalmood oo xidhmo ah oo Isbaanish ah; CDC Milestone Tracker App ee Isbaanishka; Ilaha autismka ee Sisinta ee Isbaanishka.

**Kooxaha Taageerada Waalidka:** Khadka Diiran ee Isbaanishka ee California (Sandra Alfaro, 562-622-9286); TACA kooxaha waalidka ku hadla Isbaanishka; Kooxo badan oo maxalli ah oo ku yaal CA, TX, FL, NY.

** Muddada Dhaqanka: *** "autismo" (isku-dhafka tooska ah, macnaha dhexdhexaad ah).

**Tixgelinta Dhaqanka:** Ootiisamka waxaa laga helay 2.5 sano ka dib carruurta Latino. "Familismo" waxay noqon kartaa caqabad iyo xoog labadaba. Dhaleeceynta agagaarka caafimaadka dhimirka ayaa loo arkaa calaamad daciifnimo. Caqiidada diinta ("Doorka Ilaah") ayaa laga yaabaa inay daahiyaan raadinta ogaanshaha. Cuqdad sare waxay la xiriirtaa waxbarashada waalidnimo ee hoose.

Ilaha: [PMC: Caqabadaha Ootiisamka Baadhitaannada Isbaanishka/Latinx Families] Bulshada](https://autismspectrumnews.org/navigating-autism-and-stigma-in-the-hispanic-community/)

### SHIINEYSI -- Mandarin/Cantonese (3.5M ku hadla)

** Aaladaha Sawirka:** M-CHAT-R/F nooca Shiineeska ee la ansaxiyay; Liisaska Hubinta Hal-abuurka CDC ee Shiineeska La Fududeeyay.

**Faa'iido-doon:** Ootiisamku wuxuu ku hadlaa kheyraadka Shiinaha; CPAD (Ururka Waalidiinta Shiinaha ee Naafada), la xiriir Vanda Yung (626) 307-3837; Kooxda Shaqada Caafimaadka Qoyska Aasiya.

** Waxqabadyada Dhaqan ahaan La Haboon Isha: [Springer: PTA Oo Ka Mid ah Qoysaska Soogalootiga Shiinaha](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s10826-025-03102-2)

**Shuruudaha Dhaqanka:** 自闭症 (zibizheng, "xaalad is-xiran") ama 孤独症 (guduzheng, "xaalad kelinimo") - labaduba waxay wataan tilmaamo dhaleecayn ah. Waxaa socda dadaallo lagu raadinayo erey-bixin yar oo dhaleeceyn ah.

**Tixgelinta Dhaqanka:** Konfucianism xooggan/Budhiismka/Saamaynta Taoism; "walaaca wejiga" waxay ka dhigtaa waalidiinta inay u nuglaadaan is- ceebaynta; diidmo waa caadi; soo-gaadhista deegaanka ayaa inta badan lagu eedeeyaa. Ilaha: [PMC: Aragtida Waalidka/Bixiyaha ee Dadka Shiineyska ah ee Soogalootiga ah](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC6499700/), [Dabeecada: Dhaliilaha Waalidiinta Shiinaha](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41598-072-078)

### TAGALOG/FILIPINO (1.7M ku hadla)

** Aaladaha Daawashada:** M-CHAT waa la heli karaa laakiin ma jiraan qiyaaso luqadeed oo la jaanqaadaya oo ka jira Tagalog; Xaashida Xaqiiqda Ootiisamka CDC ee Tagalog (iyada oo loo sii marayo USC UCED).

**Faa'iido-doon:** Ootiisam Pamilya (oo 2024 lagu aasaasay Jacksonville, FL); Filibiin American ururada bulshada.

** Muddada Dhaqanka:** Ma jiro Eray Tagalog oo si ballaaran loo habeeyey; Ingiriisi "Autism" caadi ahaan loo isticmaalo.

**Tixgelinta Dhaqanka:** Dhalashada ilmo naafo ah waxaa loo arki karaa "Nimco xagga Eebbe ka timid" (qaabayn togan), LAAKIIN ceebta iyo ceebaynta ayaa ka qayb qaadata in aan la wargelin. Dhismayaasha daryeelka qoyska fidsan ee xooggan. Khatarta ogaanshaha khaldan sababtoo ah la'aanta aaladaha baarista Tagalog. Ilaha: [ScholarWorks: Filipino American Cultural Beliefs](https://scholarworks.uark.edu/etd/5633/), [Inquirer: Bayani and the ABCs of Me](https://usa.inquirer.net/169791/celebrating-filipino-children-with-bayanicid-autism)

### VIETNAMESE (1.5M ku hadla)

** Aaladaha Sawirka:** M-CHAT-R/F waxaa lagu heli karaa Fiyatnaamiis; Liisaska Hubinta Hal-abuurka CDC ee Fiyatnaamiis.

**Faa'iido-doon:** VFAAB (Qoysaska Vietnamese ee ay saameeyeen Ootiisamku iyo Caqabadaha kale) -- waxay kaabaysaa godadka dhaqanka waxayna ku xidhaa adeegyada ([Seattle Children's: VFAAB](https://www.seattlechildrens.org/clinics/autism-center/the-autism-blog/vfaab-vietnamese-families-autism-journey/)); Waalidiinta Vietnamese ee qaba Ururka Carruurta Naafada ah (VPDCA), la xidhiidh Hung Gia Nguyen (714) 527-9216.

**Erayga Dhaqanka:** "benh tu ky" (病自己, "self disease") -- macnaha dhaleeceynta.

**Tixgelinta Dhaqanka:** ASD waxaa loo arkaa "cudur" uu keeno "karma" ama dhaqan xumo hore. "Waji luminta" waxay u horseedaa qoysaska inay qariyaan naafada. Carruurta Autistic-ka ah ayaa loo arkaa "daciifnimo iyo culays." Caqabada luqadda iyo xishoodku waxay gacan ka geystaan ​​ogaanshaha dambe. Fiidiyowga iyo nuxurka afka ayaa si adag loogu taliyay agab qoran. Ilaha: [USC: La noolaanshaha Ootiisamka gudaha Vietnamese American Community](https://centerforhealthjournalism.org/our-work/reporting/living-autism-vietnamese-american-community), [Tandfonline: Stigma Dhexdhexaadinta Vietnamese Waalidiinta](https://www.tandfonline.com/doi/full/10.1080/20473869.2025.2477621)

### CARABIGA (1.3M ku hadla)

** Aaladaha Sawirka:** M-CHAT-R/F waxaa lagu heli karaa Carabi; dhaqan ahaan loogu habeeyey lahjadda Carabiga ee Morocco (la ansixiyay).

** Kheyraadka Dowladda:** Warqadda Xaqiiqda Ootiisamka ee CDC LTSAE oo Carabi ah (oo loo sii marayo USC UCEDD).

**Macaash-doon:** Ootiisamku wuxuu ku hadlaa agabka luqadda Carabiga.

**Erayga Dhaqanka:** "التوحد" (al-Tawahud, "is-nuugista/go'doominta").

**Tixgelinta Dhaqanka:** Sifooyin ka sarreeya (jinni, jinni, "il xun") bulshooyinka qaarkood. Dhaqanka urur-kooxeedku wuxuu dhaleeceeyaa ka leexashada caadooyinka. 12% waalidiintu waxay ku fikiraan inay qariyaan cudurka. Dareen diidmo iyo dareen caadifadeed waa caadi. LAAKIIN dhaqanka qoyska ku jihaysan wuxuu bixiyaa taageero xooggan marka la aqbalo. Qoysas badan oo Muslimiin ah ayaa naafanimada u arka "idanka Alle," oo bixiya dhiirigelinta daryeelka. Ilaha: [Arab News: Stigma in Middle East](https://www.arabnews.com/node/1476196/middle-east), [USF: Aragtida Hooyooyinka Carabta Maraykanka](https://digitalcommons.usf.edu/cgi/viewcontent.cgi?article=8318&context=etd)

### FRENCH / HAITIAN CROOLE (1.18M + 895K ku hadla)

** Aaladaha Sawirka:** M-CHAT-R/F waxaa lagu heli karaa Faransiis iyo Creole.

**Faa'iido-doon:** Autism509 (ku xidha waalidiinta Haitian adduunka oo dhan agab); FAU/CARD (Taageerida Ootiisamka Bulshada Haitian, [FAU link](https://www.fau.edu/education/centersandprograms/card/newsevents/autism-in-the-haitian-community/)).

**Arrinta Muhiimka ah ee Akhriska:** Haiti waxay ku hadlaan Creole laakiin qaar badan ma akhriyi karaan ama ma qori karaan. Ilaha dukumeenti ku salaysan ee Creole kuma filna. Turjubaanada labada luuqad ku hadla ee shirarka IEP waxaa laga yaabaa inaysan fahmin ereybixino autism oo khaas ah Creole. Qaababka afka/muuqaalka/ maqalka waa lama huraan.

**Tixgelinta Dhaqanka:** Dhaleeceynta weyn ee ka jirta guryaha Haitian. Hooyooyinka oo si cad u hadlaya ayaa sii kordhaya (isbedelka dhaqanka). Caqabado-dhaqaale-bulsheed waxay isku daraan caqabadaha luqadda (70% cunto yari, ~ 50% caymis la'aan). Xigasho: [Haitian Times: Hooyooyinka Haitian waxay ujeedadoodu tahay inay meesha ka saaraan ceebeynta](https://haitiantimes.com/2021/04/02/haitian-moms-of-autistic-kids-aim-to-remove-stigma-get-services/)

### KUURIYA (1.1M ku hadla)

** Aaladaha Sawirka:** M-CHAT-R/F oo Kuuriyaan ah; Liisaska hubinta ee CDC Milestone ee Kuuriya.

**Waxqabadyada Dhaqanka La Qabsaday:** Waalidiinta Talaabo Qaadaya (PTA) Kuuriyaan -- barnaamij waxbarasho oo ay hogaamiyaan dadka isku faca ah oo ay bixiyaan hogaamiyayaasha asaaga ah ee soogalootiga Kuuriya ah ee korinaya caruur waa wayn oo autism-ka. Xigasho: [OAR: Taageerida Daryeel-bixiyeyaasha Soogalootiga Kuuriya](https://researchautism.org/oaracle-newsletter/supporting-korean-immigrant-caregivers-of-autistic-children/)

** Muddada Dhaqanka:** 자폐증 (japyejeung) -- dhaleeceynta macnaha la mid ah erey Shiinees ah.

**Tixgelinta Dhaqanka:** Kuuriyaanku waxay ka warbixiyaan dhaleeceynta weyn ee xagga sifooyinka autism-ka iyo kuwa Ameerikaanka ah. Ka warqabka/taageerada kala duwanaanshaha neerfayaasha Hooyooyinku waxa laga yaabaa inay diidi karaan ogaanshaha sababta oo ah summada "joogta ah iyo ceebaynta". Raaxo la'aan iyo ceebayntu waxay u horseedaa qoysaska inay ka labalabeeyaan raadinta ogaanshaha cudurka. Ilaha: [PMC: Stigma in South Korea vs US](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC9450814/), [PMC: Cultural Beliefs about Autism](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC7008392/)

### RUUSHKA (1.04M ku hadla)

**Macaash-doon:** Ootiisamku wuxuu ku hadlaa ilaha Ruushka; Khayraadka Mashruuca Autism-ka ee Philly oo af Ruush ah.

**Erayga Dhaqanka:** "аутизм" (autizm, tarjumaad ka Ingiriis).

**Tixgelinta Dhaqanka:** Caqiidooyinka caafimaadka dhimirka ee duugoobay ayaa sii hurinaya dhaleeceynta. Ootiisamku wuxuu inta badan ku wareersan yahay schizophrenia. Ka-reebista bulshadu waxay qoysaska ku riixdaa go'doon. Caqabadaha ku wajahan daryeelka caafimaadka iyo ilaha waxbarashada. Ilaha: [Adeegyada Ootiisamka Sare: Ootiisamka Ruushka](https://www.advancedautism.com/post/autism-in-russia)

### HINDI (863K ku hadla)

**Faa'iido-doon:** Hay'adaha autismka ee Hindi-gaar ah oo xaddidan oo ku yaal Mareykanka.

## Caddaynta Faragelinta Dhaqan ahaan La Qabsaday

Falanqaynta meta-2024 ayaa lagu helay **saamayn weyn, togan, oo muhiim ah** faragelinta autism-ka dhaqan ahaan ka jawaaba: natiijooyinka xidhiidhka bulshada ee muhiimka u ah heerka ilmaha, natiijooyinka caafimaadka dhimirka ee muhiimka u ah labada waalid iyo carruurta, daacadnimada waalidka ee hirgelinta istaraatiijiyada ayaa muujisay horumar la taaban karo. Dhab ahaantii, ** faragelinta dhaqan ahaan ka jawaabaya waxay keentaa natiijooyin la mid ah kuwa asalka ah ee aan la bedelin ***. Xigasho: [PMC: Falanqaynta Meta-Falanqaynta Dhaqanka Ka Jawaabta](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC11260274/)

---

## Helitaanka Halis ah: Laba luqolaha iyo Ootiisamka

Laba-luqaduhu ** wax saamayn taban ah kuma laha** horumarka luqadda ee carruurta qabta ASD. Iyadoo ay taasi jirto, waalidiinta badankoodu waxay soo sheegaan in dhakhaatiirtu ay kula taliyeen in aan la bixin deegaan laba luqole ah. Appku waa inuu si firfircoon ula dagaallamo khuraafaadkan oo ku dhiirigeliyaa qoysaska inay ilaashadaan afkooda hooyo. Isha: [PMC: Laba-luqadnimo ee Cudurka Ootiisamka](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC7370402/)

## Mabaadi'da Muhiimka ah ee Nakhshadeynta App-ka

1. U adeegso xubnaha bulshada sidii dillaal dhaqameed -- faragelinta asaaga ayaa aad loo aamini karaa
2. Samee autism-ka gudaha qiyamka dhaqameed ee jira, ma aha sida fikradda reer galbeedka lagu soo rogay
3. Ixtiraam dhismaha qoyska -- waxaa ka mid ah qoyska ballaaran xagga waxbarashada
4. Si toos ah uga hadal ceebaha laakiin si xasaasi ah -- caadi uga dhig sheekooyinka
5. Bixi xiriir bilow ah oo qarsoodi ah -- u ogolow waalidiinta inay sahamiyaan iyagoon la kulmin
6. Ka faa'iidayso qaab-dhismeedka diineed/Ruuxa halka ay ku habboon tahay
7. Bixi qaabab badan oo ka kooban: muuqaal, maqal, qoraal, sawiro
8. Ka wakiil ah qoysaska kala duwan dhammaan waxyaabaha muuqaalka ah
9. Si daacad ah u qir caqabadaha -- heerka socdaalka, caymiska, luqadda
10. Dhis kalsoonida ka hor macluumaadka -- Kalsoonida bulshadu waa inay ka horraysa waxbarashada caafimaadka

## Caqabadaha Gaarka ah ee Soogalootiga (Isdhaafsiga Dhaqanka)

Cilmi-baadhistu waxay si joogto ah u caddaysaa caqabadahan dhammaan bulshooyinka soogalootiga ah:
1. Caqabadaha luqadda (lambarka xannibaadda)
2. Xog la'aanta agabka la heli karo
3. Kala duwanaanta dhaqanka ee fahamka marxaladaha horumarka
4. Cabsida xaaladda socdaalka -- ka fogaanshaha nidaamka dawladda/caafimaadka
5. Caqabadaha dhaqan-dhaqaale -- faqri, caymis la'aan, cunto yari
6. Go'doominta bulshada -- luminta isku-xidhka taageerada qoyska ballaaran
7. Culayska la qabsiga -- maaraynta autismka iyadoo la qabsanaysa waddan cusub
8. Kakanaanta hagidda -- daryeelka caafimaadka/nidaamyada waxbarashadu waa jahawareer
9. Karti la'aanta dhaqanka bixiyaha -- ogaanshaha khaldan, eexda, cayrinta
10. Kalsooni darada hay'adaha

Xigasho muhim ah oo cilmi baaris ah: **"Hadii aan soo galooti nahay dhamaanteen waxaan ku lunnay safarkayaga autism-ka"** -- Source: [ScienceDirect: Experiences of Immigrant Fathers](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S3050656525000604)
