## 1. M-CHAT-R/F (Liiska hubinta ee Ootiisamka ee saanqaadyada, dib loo eegay iyada oo dabagal lagu sameeyay)

**Magaca buuxa:** Liiska hubinta ee Ootiisamka oo wax laga beddelay, dib loo eegay iyada oo dabagal lagu sameeyay

**Da'da La daboolay:** 16-30 bilood

**Nooca:** Qalabka baadhista warbixinta-waalid heerka 1 (2-marxaladda: xog-ururinta 20-ka shay ee bilowga ah + waraysiga daba-galka ah ee wanaagga)

**Qiimaha/Shatiga:** BILAASH u ah daawaynta, cilmi-baadhista, iyo ujeedooyinka waxbarashada. Waxaa laga heli karaa bilaash [mchatscreen.com] (https://www.mchatscreen.com/)

**Dareenka & Gaarkanaanta (Daraasadaha Muhiimka ah):**
- ** Ansixinta asalka ah (Robins et al., 2014, *Dhakhaatiirta Carruurta*, 133(1), 37-45):** N=16,071 saanqaadayaal ah oo la baaray 18-iyo 24-bilood booqashooyinka wanaagsan ee ilmaha. Carruurta keenaysa>=3 bilawgii iyo>=2 dabagal kadib waxay haysteen 47.5% PPV ee ogaanshaha ASD (95% CI: 0.41-0.54) iyo 94.6% PPV dib u dhac/walaac kasta oo korriin ah. Dareenka: 0.83-0.85; Gaar ahaan: 0.95-0.99.
  - Isha: [Robins et al., 2014 - PMC](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC3876182/)
- ** Falanqaynta Meta (Yuen et al., 2023, *Dhakhaatiirta Carruurta*, 151(6)):** 15 daraasadood, 18 muunado, 10 waddan, 49,841 carruur ah. Dareenka dhegta: 82.6% (95% CI 76.2-88.9); Gaar ahaan: 45.7% (95% CI 25.0-66.4); PPV: 57.7% (95% CI 48.6-66.8). PPV waxay ku badan tahay shaybaarada khatarta sare leh (75.6%) vs. khatarta hoose (51.2%).
  - Isha: [Meta-analysis - AAP Pediatrics](https://publications.aap.org/pediatrics/article/151/6/e2022059393/191300/Meta-analysis-of-the-Modified-Checklist-for-Autism)
- ** JAMA Dib-u-eegis nidaamsan ee Dhakhaatiirta Carruurta (2023):** Dareen-celinta 0.83 (95% CI 0.77-0.88); Gaar ahaan 0.94 (95% CI 0.89-0.97).
  - Isha: [JAMA Pediatrics 2023](https://jamanetwork.com/journals/jamapediatrics/fullarticle/2801663)
- ** ansaxinta Singapore 2024 (Zheng et al.):** N=5,336 carruur ah. Dareenka 88.6%; Gaar ahaan 71.4%; PPV 90.7%.
  - Xigasho: [Zheng et al., 2024](https://journals.sagepub.com/doi/10.1177/13623613231205748)
- ** Ansixinta Isbaanishka:** Dareenka 0.79; Gaar ahaan 0.99; PPV 0.39; NPV 0.99.
  - Isha: [PubMed 30328577](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/30328577/)

**Luqadaha (58+ tarjumaad):** Carabi, Shiinees (La fududeeyay & Dhaqan), Czech, Dutch, French, French Canadian, German, Greek, Gujarati, Hindi, Icelandic, Italian, Japanese, Kannada, Korean, Kurdish, Malaay, Portuguese, Ruush, Sinhala, Somali, Isbaanish, Iswidish, Tagalog, Tamil, Turkish, Vietnamese, Urdu, iyo qaar kaloo badan.
- **Dhammaan 10ka luqadood ee ugu sarreeya ee soogalootiga Mareykanka ayaa daboolay:** Isbaanish, Shiinaha/Mandarin, Tagalog, Fiyatnaamiis, Carabi, Faransiis, Kuuriyaan, Ruush, Boortaqiis, Hindi.
- Ka soo deji dhammaan tarjumaada: [M-CHAT Translations Bogga](https://www.mchatscreen.com/mchat-rf/translations/)

** Hirgelinta Dijital ah:**
- Nooca dhijitaalka ah ee rasmiga ah [mchatscreen.com] (https://www.mchatscreen.com/)
- [Autism Speaks online screener](https://www.autismspeaks.org/screen-your-child)
- [ABS Kids M-CHAT-R qalab online](https://www.abskids.com/mchat/)
- [Baareeyaha khadka tooska ah ee Xarunta Dhaqanka Action] (https://www.actionbehavior.com/screener/m-chat)
- Hirgelinta dhijitaalka ah ee ku salaysan tablet-ka oo uu bartay Sturner et al. (2017): Kordhinta dukumeenti EMR saxda ah laga bilaabo 54% ilaa 92%, iyo ficilka ku haboon ee shaashadaha togan min 25% ilaa 85%.
  - Isha: [Sturner et al., 2017 - PMC](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC5397992/)

** Helitaanka Farqiga Muhiimka ah: *** Qoysaska waxbarashada hooyada hoose iyo kuwa laga tirada badan yahay waxay muujinayaan sicir-bararka bilowga ah ee- togan iyo ka qaybqaadashada hoose ee dabagalka. Waraysiga daba-galka ahi waa lama huraan si loo dhimo heerka beenta ah ee beenta ah (PPV waxay ka booday .36 ilaa .74 iyada oo la socota).
- Isha: [Caqabadaha Sociodemographic - PMC](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC4442731/)

** Da'da La daboolay:** Dhalashada ilaa 8 sano

**Qiimaha/Shatiga:** LISASKA LEH
- Waalidiinta gaarka ah: $19.95 baadhistiiba [PEDStest Online](https://pedstestonline.com/)
- Bixiyeyaasha: qaabka shatiga oo leh tijaabooyin bilaash ah oo loo heli karo daryeelka aasaasiga ah, Head Start, dugsiyada barbaarinta, rugaha caafimaadka ee dugsiga ku salaysan, NICU/EI dabagal, iyo cilmi baaris
-PEDS Online waxaa ka mid ah is dhexgalka M-CHAT-R
- Isha: [PEDStest.com](https://pedstest.com/)

**Dareenka & Gaarka ah:**

Shaashadda gaarka ah ee ASD ahaan vs. M-CHAT: Dareenka 77.8%, laakiin gaar ahaan 26.3% -- Ma aha beddel wanaagsan M-CHAT marka si gaar ah loo baadho ASD
  - Isha: [PEDS iyo ASQ isbarbardhigga - PMC](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC2764374/)

**Luqadaha (60+ tarjumaad):** Albanian, Amxaari, Carabi, Armenian, Bengali, Bulgaariya, Burma, Cambodian, Shiinees (Dhaqameed & La Fududeeyay), Koongo Swahili, Czech, Danish, Dutch, Dzongkha, Farsi, Filibiin/Tagalog, Faransiis, Faransiis Kanadiyaan, Galician, Jarmal, Greek, Hindi, Hibraaniga, Haitian, Iceland Karen, Kuuriyaan, Laotian, Malay, Nepali, Polish, Portuguese, Cape Verdean, Punjabi, Quechua, Ruush, Serbian (Cyrillic & Latin), Samoan, Soomaali, Sotho, Swahili, Swedish, Tagalog, Taiwanese, Tamil, Thai, Turkish, Urdu, Vietnamese, Visayan, Yoruba, Zulu, iyo kuwo kale.

## 4. SCQ (Su'aalaha Isgaadhsiinta Bulshada)

**Magac buuxa:** Su'aalo-ururinta Isgaadhsiinta Bulshada

** Da'da la daboolay: *** 4 sano iyo ka weyn (da'da maskaxeed ee ka weyn 2 sano)

**Nooca:** Baadhiyaha warbixinta waalidka heerka 2. Laba nooc: Hadda (3 bilood ee u dambeeyay) iyo Cimriga (taariikhda korriinka oo dhammaystiran). 40 haa/maya shay. Iyada oo ku saleysan walxaha ADI-R.

**Qiimaha/Shatiga:** LICENSED, oo ay daabacday WPS (Adeegyada Cilmi-nafsiga ee Galbeedka)
- Qalabka waxaa ku jira buug-gacmeed, 20 Foomamka AutoScore ee Hadda, 20 Foomamka AutoScore Lifetime
- Marka la barbar dhigo qalabka ogaanshaha (qiyaastii ~ $150-250 xirmo ku salaysan xogta tafaariiqda caalamiga ah)
- Waxaa laga heli karaa onlayn iyada oo loo marayo Nidaamka Qiimaynta Khadka Tooska ah ee WPS (OES)
- Isha: [WPS SCQ Page](https://www.wpspublish.com/scq-social-communication-questionnaire.html)

**Dareenka & Gaarka ah:**
- Ansixinta asalka ah (Berument et al., 1999): Gooyn>=15: Dareenka 0.85, Gaarka ah 0.75 ee ASD vs. non-ASD
- Rutter, Bailey & Lord (2003): ASD vs. non-ASD: Dareenka 0.88, Gaarka ah 0.72; Ootiisamka vs. Autism-ka: Dareenka 0.90, Gaar ahaan 0.86
- Carruurta <4 sano jir: Dareenka iyo gaarnimada ayaa hooseeya oo aan la isku hallayn karin
  - Isha: [CHOP SCQ dulmar](https://www.research.chop.edu/car-autism-roadmap/social-communication-questionnaire-scq) | [SCQ ee carruurta <4 - PMC](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC5684265/)
- Ansixinta Nayjeeriya (Nwokolo, 2024): Goynta 10: Dareenka 0.81, Gaar ahaan 0.88, AUC 0.83
  - Xigasho: [Nwokolo 2024 - Wiley](https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/full/10.1002/aur.3038)

**Luqadaha:** Danish, Dutch, English, Finnish, German, Hebrew, Hungarian, Icelandic, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Norwegian, Romanian, Russian, Spanish, Swedish
- ** Waxay daboolaysaa 4 ka mid ah 10ka luqadood ee ugu sarreeya ee soogalootiga Mareykanka:** Isbaanish, Kuuriyaan, Ruush, Boortaqiis (lama xaqiijin)

## 6. BAABUURTA-2 (Miisaanka Qiimaynta Ootiisamka Carruurnimada, Daabacaadda Labaad)

**Magaca buuxa:** Qiyaasta Qiimaynta Ootiisamka Carruurnimada, Daabacaadda Labaad

** Da'da la daboolay: *** 2 sano iyo ka weyn

**Nooca:** Miisaanka indha-indhaynta-u qiimeeyay dhakhtarka. Laba nooc: CARS2-ST (Standard, kuwa leh IQ <80 ama isgaarsiinta <6 yrs) iyo CARS2-HF (High-Functioning, kuwa leh IQ>= 80 iyo isgaarsiinta faseexa ah). Sidoo kale waxaa ku jira CARS2-QPC (Su'aalaha Waalidiinta/Daryeelayaasha).

**Qiimaha/Shatiga:** LISASKA LEH. Waxaa daabacay WPS.
- Xidhmada dhamaystiran waxa ku jira buug-gacmeedka, 25 Buug-yaraha Qiimaynta ST, 25 Buugaagta Qiimaynta HF, 25 foomamka QPC
- Qiimaha gaarka ah ee Maraykanka oo aan si guud loo qorin; toos ula xiriir WPS.
- Isha: [WPS CARS-2 Bogga](https://www.wpspublish.com/cars-2-childhood-autism-rating-scale-second-edition.html)

**Dareenka & Gaarka ah:**
- Dhibcaha CARS2-ST ee 30: 100% dareenka, 84% gaar ahaan, 97% PPV, 100% NPV (ka soo horjeeda ADOS-2)
  - Isha: [CARS-2 vs ADOS-2 - PMC](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC9816003/)
- Guud ahaan: Dareenka 81-100%, Gaar ahaan 70-100% dhammaan noocyada
- Ansixinta Kuuriya (K-CARS2): ST: Dareenka 100%, Gaar ahaan 100%; HF: Dareenka 100%, Gaar ahaan 96%
  - Isha: [CARS-2 ansaxinta Kuuriya](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S1750946723000284)
Ansixinta iiraan (2025): Isku halaynta wanaagsan, ansaxnimada, iyo awooda takoorka ee CARS2-HF
  - Isha: [CARS-2 Iraan - PMC](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC11928914/)

**Luqadaha:** Lagu ansixiyay Kuuriya, iiraan/Farsi, iyo kuwa kale. Turjumaada la daabacay aan si dhamaystiran loo taxnayn.

## 10. AQ (Autism Spectrum Quotient)

**Magaca buuxa:** Otiism Spectrum Quotient

- AQ-gaaban (28 walxood): Dhibcaha>65: xasaasiga 0.97, Gaar ahaan 0.82

- Qaangaarka AQ (muunad kale): Dareenka 0.77, Gaar ahaan 0.29 (ka hooseeya muunadaha bulshada)

**Luqadaha:** Waxaa lagu heli karaa "luqadaha badankooda" marka loo eego Xarunta Cilmi-baarista Ootiisamka. Turjumaada la diiwaangeliyay waxaa ka mid ah Jabbaan, Shiine/Mandarin, Kuuriyaan, Faransiis, Talyaani, Nederlaan, Iswidish, Malay/Bahasa Malaysia, iyo kuwo kale oo badan. ## 14. Tixgelinta Dhaqanka ee Baarista Ootiisamka

### Soo Bandhigidda Calaamadaha Dhaqan-Dhaqameed

** Xidhiidh isha: *** La'aanta indho la'aanta waxaa lagu sifeeyay DSM-5 inay tahay calaamada ASD ee iman karta, laakiin dhaqamo badan oo Aasiya iyo Afrikaan ah, xidhiidhka indhaha ee shakhsiyaadka maamulka ayaa loo arkaa ixtiraam darro. Tani waxay abuurtaa eexda cabbirka aasaasiga ah.

**Kala duwanaanshaha warbixinta waalidka:** Hooyooyinka Latino waxay soo sheegeen calaamadaha ASD aad uga yar marka loo eego hooyooyinka Anglo-ga, xitaa marka caruurtoodu ay sare u kaceen ADOS darnaanta ASD. Waalidiinta cadaanka ahi waxay xooga saaraan cilladaha isgaadhsiinta ("ilmahaygu kama jawaabo magacooda") halka waalidiinta madow ay u qaabaysan karaan habdhaqan ("ilmahaygu igama daayo"), taasoo keenaysa fasiraado caafimaad oo kala duwan.
- Isha: [qaabka fikradeed - PMC](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC7614360/)

## Talooyinka muhiimka ah ee App-ka Hackathon

Iyada oo ku saleysan cilmi-baaristan, habka ugu saameynta badan ee waalidiinta muhaajiriinta ah ayaa mudnaanta siin doona:

1. **M-CHAT-R/F oo ah mishiinka baadhista xudunta u ah *** -- Waa bilaash, lagu ansaxiyay 58+ luqadood oo daboolaya dhammaan 10ka luqadood ee soogalootiga Maraykanka ugu sarreeya, waxay leedahay saldhigga caddaynta ugu weyn, waxaana mar horeba AAP kula taliyay baadhista caalamiga ah ee 18 iyo 24 bilood.

2. **Q-CHAT-10 oo ah shaashad degdeg ah oo dheeri ah *** -- Bilaash, 10 shay, dareen aad u xoog badan/gaar ah, oo u fiican hubin bilawga degdega ah.

3. **CAST ee carruurta da'da dugsiga** -- Bilaash ah, si fiican loo ansixiyay, waxay dabooshaa farqiga da'da 4-11 ee M-CHAT aysan sameyn.

4. **AQ loogu talagalay carruurta waaweyn/dhallinyarada/dadka waaweyn *** -- Bilaash, oo lagu heli karo luqadaha intooda badan, waxay dabooshaa cimriga buuxa.

5. **ASDQ oo ah raad-raaca calaamadaha il-furan *** -- Bilaash, cusub, si wanaagsan loo ansixiyey, ayaa lagu dhex dari karaa la socodka socda.

6. ** CDC Milestone Tracker integration *** -- Khayraadka bilaashka ah ee lagu heli karo 12 luqadood oo loogu talagalay la socodka korriinka 2 bilood ilaa 5 sano.

7. **Lakabka la qabsiga dhaqanka *** -- Halis ah marka la eego farqiga la diiwaan geliyay. App-ka waa inuu ku jiraa qaabaynta su'aalaha dhaqan ahaan xasaasiga ah, nuxurka waxbarasho ee ka hadlaya ceebaynta, iyo hagida xisaabaadka caadooyinka dhaqameed ee ku xeeran xidhiidhka indhaha, dhaqanka bulshada, iyo qaabdhismeedka go'aan qaadashada qoyska.

8. **Is-dhexgalka dariiqa dabagalka *** -- Wareysiga dabagalka ee M-CHAT-R/F waa lama huraan (PPV waxay ka boodaa 36% ilaa 74% iyadoo la raacayo), markaa dhisidda socodka daba-galka tooska ah ama la hagayo waxay muhiim u tahay yaraynta faa'iidooyinka been abuurka ah, gaar ahaan dadka laga tirada badan yahay iyo kuwa dakhligoodu yar yahay.
