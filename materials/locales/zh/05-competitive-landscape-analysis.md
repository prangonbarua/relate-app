## 1. 自闭症筛查应用程序
|||九月|||
### Canvas Dx（以前称为 Cognoa）
- **平台：** iOS（仅限处方，临床医生管理）
- **公司：** Cognoa, Inc.
- **FDA 状态：** FDA De Novo 授权（2021 年）——第一个也是唯一一个获得 FDA 授权的自闭症诊断辅助工具
- **目标年龄：** 18 个月至 72 个月
- **工作原理：** 机器学习算法结合临床医生评估和护理人员问卷分析家长上传的儿童行为视频
- **准确度：** 对于获得确定输出的 32%：敏感性 98.4%，特异性 78.9%，PPV 80.8%，NPV 98.3%
- **成本：** 每次评估 2,280 美元（批发）；一些保险公司承保（例如 Highmark）
- **语言：** 英语
- **移民家庭支持：** 无 -- 仅英语，需要临床医生处方，极其昂贵
- **优点：** FDA授权、准确度高、潜在的保险范围
- **缺点：** 非常昂贵，需要临床医生，只有 32% 得到确定的结果，语言支持有限
|||九月|||
### EarliPoint 健康
- **平台：** 基于平板电脑的专有设备（由临床医生管理）
- **FDA 状态：** FDA 510(k) 批准（2022 年）
- **目标年龄：** 16-30 个月
- **工作原理：** 眼动追踪技术在孩子观看视频时测量视觉注意力生物标记； 20 分钟内完成
- **准确度：** 78% 敏感性，85.4% 特异性
- **费用：** 自付费用 599 美元；乔治亚州医疗补助涵盖了它；可用的付款计划
- **语言：** 可能仅限英语
- **移民家庭支持：** 有限 -- 需要去诊所、费用障碍、语言不清楚
- **优点：** 适用于幼儿（16 个月），见效快，经 FDA 批准
- **缺点：** 仅供临床医生使用，不是消费者应用程序，可用性有限
|||九月|||
### AS检测
- **平台：** iOS 和 Android（免费，面向消费者）
- **公司：** 澳大利亚拉筹伯大学
- **目标年龄：** 12、18 和 24 个月
- **如何运作：** 视频引导家长评估；家长观看自闭症和非自闭症儿童展示里程碑的视频，然后回答问题
- **准确度：** 83%
- **费用：** 免费
- **语言：** 英语、普通话、斯洛伐克语、西班牙语
- **移民家庭支持：** 中等 - 4 种语言，包括西班牙语和普通话，免费
- **优点：** 免费、视频指导、多种语言、有研究支持
- **缺点：** 仅筛查（非诊断）、仅限 24 个月、语言有限、一些假阴性
|||九月|||
### CDC 里程碑跟踪器
- **平台：** iOS 和 Android（免费）
- **公司：** 美国疾病预防控制中心
- **目标年龄：** 2 个月至 5 岁
- **工作原理：** 社交/情感、语言、认知和运动里程碑的交互式插图清单； 18 个月和 24 个月时的自闭症筛查提醒
- **费用：** 免费
- **语言：** 仅英语和西班牙语
- **移民家庭支持：** 有限——尽管是政府应用程序，但只有 2 种语言
- **优点：** 政府支持、免费、开发范围广泛、易于使用
- **缺点：** 只有英语/西班牙语、一般里程碑（非自闭症特定）、可操作性有限
|||九月|||
### M-CHAT-R/F（数字版）
- **平台：** 基于网络（多个提供商提供免费的在线版本）
- **如何运作：** 针对 16-30 个月幼儿的 20 个问题是/否家长问卷；具有自动风险评分功能的数字版本
- **费用：** 免费
- **语言：** 纸质表格翻译成 50 多种语言；数字版本主要是英语/西班牙语
- **移民家庭支持：** 纸面上很好，数字上很差——底层工具被广泛翻译，但数字实施大多是英语
- **优点：** 黄金标准筛选工具，经过广泛验证，广泛翻译
- **缺点：** 数字版本的语言有限；仅文本（无视频指导）；需要识字

## 1. AUTISM SCREENING APPS

### Canvas Dx (formerly Cognoa)
- **Platform:** iOS (prescription-only, clinician-administered)
- **Company:** Cognoa, Inc.
- **FDA Status:** FDA De Novo authorized (2021) -- FIRST AND ONLY FDA-authorized diagnostic aid for autism
- **Target Age:** 18 months to 72 months
- **How It Works:** Machine learning algorithms analyze videos of child behavior uploaded by parents, combined with clinician assessments and caregiver questionnaires
- **Accuracy:** For the 32% who received a determinate output: sensitivity 98.4%, specificity 78.9%, PPV 80.8%, NPV 98.3%
- **Cost:** $2,280 per evaluation (wholesale); some insurers cover it (e.g., Highmark)
- **Languages:** English
- **Immigrant Family Support:** NONE -- English-only, requires clinician prescription, extremely expensive
- **Strengths:** FDA authorization, high accuracy, potential insurance coverage
- **Weaknesses:** Very expensive, requires clinician, only 32% receive determinate result, limited language support

### EarliPoint Health
- **Platform:** Proprietary tablet-based device (clinician-administered)
- **FDA Status:** FDA 510(k) cleared (2022)
- **Target Age:** 16-30 months
- **How It Works:** Eye-tracking technology measuring visual attention biomarkers while child watches video; completed in under 20 minutes
- **Accuracy:** 78% sensitivity, 85.4% specificity
- **Cost:** $599 out-of-pocket; Georgia Medicaid covers it; payment plans available
- **Languages:** Likely English-only
- **Immigrant Family Support:** LIMITED -- requires clinic visit, cost barrier, language unclear
- **Strengths:** Works on very young children (16 months), quick, FDA-cleared
- **Weaknesses:** Clinician-only, not a consumer app, limited availability

### ASDetect
- **Platform:** iOS and Android (free, consumer-facing)
- **Company:** La Trobe University, Australia
- **Target Age:** 12, 18, and 24 months
- **How It Works:** Video-guided parent assessment; parents watch videos of autistic and non-autistic children demonstrating milestones, then answer questions
- **Accuracy:** 83%
- **Cost:** FREE
- **Languages:** English, Mandarin Chinese, Slovak, Spanish
- **Immigrant Family Support:** MODERATE -- 4 languages including Spanish and Mandarin, free
- **Strengths:** Free, video-guided, multiple languages, research-backed
- **Weaknesses:** Only screening (not diagnostic), only to 24 months, limited languages, some false negatives

### CDC Milestone Tracker
- **Platform:** iOS and Android (free)
- **Company:** U.S. CDC
- **Target Age:** 2 months to 5 years
- **How It Works:** Interactive illustrated checklists for social/emotional, language, cognitive, and movement milestones; autism screening reminders at 18 and 24 months
- **Cost:** FREE
- **Languages:** English and Spanish ONLY
- **Immigrant Family Support:** LIMITED -- only 2 languages despite being a government app
- **Strengths:** Government-backed, free, broad developmental range, easy to use
- **Weaknesses:** Only English/Spanish, general milestones (not autism-specific), limited actionability

### M-CHAT-R/F (Digital Versions)
- **Platform:** Web-based (multiple providers offer free online versions)
- **How It Works:** 20-question yes/no parent questionnaire for toddlers 16-30 months; digital versions with automated risk scoring
- **Cost:** FREE
- **Languages:** Paper form translated into 50+ languages; digital versions primarily English/Spanish
- **Immigrant Family Support:** GOOD on paper, POOR digitally -- the underlying tool is widely translated but digital implementations are mostly English
- **Strengths:** Gold-standard screening tool, extensively validated, widely translated
- **Weaknesses:** Digital versions limited in languages; text-only (no video guidance); requires literacy

### 自闭症导航器（我的自闭症导航器）
- **平台：** iOS 应用程序 + 网络课程
- **公司：** 佛罗里达州立大学自闭症研究所
- **目标年龄：** 1-8 岁儿童的家庭
- **如何运作：** 互动课程，包含大量视频片段，显示早期迹象和循证实践
- **成本：** 各不相同
- **语言：** 英语（初级）
- **移民家庭支持：** 有限
|||九月|||
### NIH/杜克大学数字筛选工具
- **状态：** 研究/实验（不可商用）
- **工作原理：** 平板电脑在孩子观看视频时跟踪眼睛注视、头部运动、表情、眨眼
- **准确性：** 约 90% 的后来诊断为 ASD 的儿童被正确识别； >80% 特异性

### What is MISSING:

**1. No single app combines screening + education + navigation + community for immigrant parents.** Parents must download 5-10 different apps for different needs, each with its own account and learning curve.

**2. No app provides culturally-adapted autism education in native languages.** Autism Speaks has multilingual PDFs but no interactive, culturally-sensitive app. Cultural beliefs about autism (causes, stigma, treatment) are not addressed by ANY app.

**3. No app helps immigrant parents navigate the US healthcare/education system for autism.** IEP process, Early Intervention, insurance, Medicaid -- all critical but no app explains this in multiple languages. Undivided exists but is English-only and expensive.

**4. No app addresses immigration-specific fears.** Fear of government systems (ICE/deportation), fear of labeling a child (cultural stigma), and no app explains rights regardless of immigration status.

**5. No app provides community connection in native languages.** Reddit/Wrong Planet are English-only. WhatsApp groups exist but are invisible and unfindable. No platform connects immigrant autism parents by language and culture.

## 11. 针对移民家庭的调查结果

### 诊断差异

## 11. IMMIGRANT FAMILY-SPECIFIC FINDINGS

### Diagnosis Disparities
- **Latino children** diagnosed **2.5 YEARS LATER** than white non-Latino children

### Three Categories of Barriers (Research-Validated)
